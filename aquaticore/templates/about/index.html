About aquaticore

Aquaticore will become home to a user-driven compendium of aquarium knowledge, including detailed wiki-like pages containing all pertinent knowledge about fish, plants, substrates, products, equipment, etc. From fish cures to how to choose a filter, aquaticore.com will have you covered. Expected launch date is Sept 2008. For more information or if you wish to donate time and/or resources, please contact dave@aquaticore.com
